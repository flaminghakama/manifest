We'll present material we've workshopped and recorded at the Flaming Hakama studio throughout the year. This includes some Afro-Cuban material from the alt.musica repertoire, arrangements of Brahms, classic Jazz ballads, and cues written for film & TV. There are two Afro-Cuban and Jazz-based originals, an arrangement of Brahms piano rhapsody
